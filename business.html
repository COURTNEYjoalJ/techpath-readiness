The problem

Learning is harder when the next step is unclear

Many students, graduates and career switchers struggle because they do not know which skills they are missing, what to learn first, how to build job-ready projects, how to prepare a portfolio or how to move from learning to applications.

Skill gaps

Learners often need a clearer view of weak areas before choosing the next topic.

Learning order

A roadmap helps avoid random study and turns broad goals into practical steps.

Portfolio direction

Beginner projects need enough structure to show problem, method and result.

Application support

Moving from learning to job applications requires CV, LinkedIn and tracker discipline.

The solution

An AI-ready readiness platform with practical support

TechPath Readiness helps users understand their gaps, follow a clear roadmap and prepare for practical next steps. Future AI-powered roadmap and readiness features are planned, while the current beta combines a live static platform with limited manual support.

FAQ

Clear expectations for beta support

Is TechPath a tutoring website?

It includes tutoring-style support, but the goal is a readiness platform with diagnostics, roadmaps, reports, and career guidance.

Do you guarantee jobs or visas?

No. TechPath provides learning guidance, career-readiness support, and structured preparation. It does not guarantee employment, grades, interviews, visa outcomes, or immigration advice.

How do I book support?

At this beta stage, users can request support through the contact page or LinkedIn. There is no automated booking, payment or calendar system on the website.

Is this suitable for complete beginners?

Yes, it is designed for beginners, students, graduates, and career switchers.

Will AI features be added?

Future versions plan to add AI-assisted readiness diagnostics, personalised roadmaps, and project roadmap generation.
